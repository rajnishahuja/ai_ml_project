# Stage 4 Decision Log

Append-only durable record of Stage 4 design decisions across sessions.
Format: `YYYY-MM-DD | <decision name> | <chosen option> | <one-sentence rationale>`

2026-04-30 | Zero-clause score handling | final_score=0.0 with note="no clauses assessed" surfaced in ScoreBreakdown and rendered into the PDF/DOCX score section | A 0.0 score on an empty contract is indistinguishable from a 0.0 score on a low-risk contract without an explicit note; readers of the rendered report must not assume "low risk" when the assessor saw no clauses.
2026-04-30 | Pattern deriver tier-2 LLM trigger | Gated behind config flag pattern_deriver.use_llm_tier2 (env override STAGE4_PATTERN_DERIVE_USE_LLM), default true, fires whenever tier-1 returns unknown_pattern, cached by content hash, every invocation logged at INFO with clause_id and resulting pattern code | Lets us cheaply disable the LLM fallback under rate-limit pressure or measure tier-1-only coverage during evaluation, without removing the capability.
2026-04-30 | Renderer file naming | output/<document_id>/<document_id>.{json,md,pdf,docx} (subdir per document, no timestamp suffix, overwrite on re-run) | Subdir keeps multi-document runs organized and gives a clean home for future per-document artifacts (cache snapshots, intermediate digests) without polluting top-level output; canonical version is the latest run, older versions live in git history.
2026-04-30 | Fixture realism | Synthesized CUAD-style fixture covering ≥8 clauses across Indemnification, Anti-Assignment, Change of Control, Liquidated Damages, Renewal Term, Cap On Liability, Governing Law, and an Irrevocable/Perpetual License variant, with mixed risk levels, at least one overridden=true, at least one confidence<0.6, and at least one cross_references entry | Goal is to exercise Stage 4 paths (override flag, low-confidence rendering, cross-ref display) rather than validate Stage 3 output; no real Stage 3 dump is available.
2026-04-30 | Gemini model selection | gemini-2.5-flash as default in configs/stage4_config.yaml, overridable via GEMINI_MODEL env var | Newer flash is meaningfully better at short structured-prose generation (per-clause explanations, executive digest summary), still free-tier; we are at the start of integration so any model-change cost is paid once now, and the env var provides a clean fallback if anything regresses.
